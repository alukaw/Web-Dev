Things you need to see in the city

  1. Akihabara

    Major shopping district for video games, anime, manga, electronics and computer-related goods.

  2. Tokyo Skytree

    The most recognizable tower

  3. Shibya crossing

    It has been described as the world's busiest pedestrian crossing, with as many as 3,000 people crossing during a single green light cycle.
