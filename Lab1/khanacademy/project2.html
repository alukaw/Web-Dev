Recipe #2: Omelette

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 1 tsp
Milk 2 tbsp
Eggs 2 piece
Salt 1/2 tbsp

Step 3: Cook the eggs until ready.

Recipe #3: Ramen

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Ramen noodles 1 pack
Water 2 cups
Egg 1
Soy sauce 1 tbsp
Green onion To taste

Step 1: Boil water.

Step 2: Add noodles and cook.

Step 3: Add egg and soy sauce.

Step 4: Serve with green onion.
